Welcome to Kandy

Kandy, the last royal capital of Sri Lanka, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site nestled in the hills of the Central Province. This sacred city is home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, one of Buddhism's most revered sites.

Surrounded by mountains and centered around the beautiful Kandy Lake, this cultural gem offers a perfect blend of history, spirituality, and natural beauty. Experience traditional dance, explore lush botanical gardens, and immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Sri Lankan culture.
